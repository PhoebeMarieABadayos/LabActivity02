Badminton

  • A racket sport played by either two opposing players or two opposing pairs separated between a net.
  • Objective is to hit the shuttlecock with a racket so it passes over the net and lands in the opposing team's side.
  • Fouls happen when a player tocuhes the net or hits the shuttlecock twice.
  • Invented by the Duke of Beaufort in 1873.
